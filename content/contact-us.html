FAQ

What makes your software process different?

Our approach is built on a refined blend of agile methodologies, user-centric design, and meticulous testing. We focus on frequent communication, transparent progress updates, and a commitment to quality at every development stage—ensuring the final product meets and exceeds expectations.

For how long are you in the market?

We have been delivering top-tier design and development solutions for over a decade. This extensive experience enables us to adapt swiftly to evolving technologies and industry best practices.

What info is needed to start an estimate process?

We typically need an overview of your project goals, desired features, target audience, budget range, and timeline. Any existing design assets or technical specifications also help us provide an accurate and detailed proposal.

What is a typical size of a team for a project?

Team size varies based on the scope and complexity of the project. However, most engagements involve a dedicated group of around 5–8 experts, including designers, developers, and QA specialists.

Do you provide some kind of after release support?

Yes. We offer ongoing maintenance, updates, and technical support to ensure your product remains secure, up-to-date, and fully optimized after launch.
