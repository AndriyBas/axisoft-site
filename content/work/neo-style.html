CHALLENGE‍

NeoStyle, an eCommerce brand specializing in minimalist, high-end home decor, struggled to convert website visitors into loyal buyers. Their outdated platform lacked modern design elements and personalized recommendations, leading to a less engaging shopping experience.

‍

SOLUTION‍

We overhauled NeoStyle’s online store with a clean, modern UI and seamless user journey. Integrating AI-driven product recommendations ensured customers received personalized suggestions, enhancing both satisfaction and overall brand perception.

‍

RESULT‍

NeoStyle saw a noticeable boost in conversion rates and a marked increase in average order value. The platform’s fresh design and intelligent features solidified NeoStyle’s reputation for premium home decor, driving sales growth and repeat business.
